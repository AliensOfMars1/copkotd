Welcome

There's a Place
For You Here

At The Church of Pentecost KOTD, we believe that everyone has a story and a purpose. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church to call home, you are welcome here.

We're a community of believers committed to loving God, loving people, and serving our nation. No matter your background, doubts, or struggles come as you are.
